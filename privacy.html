Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 4, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Claude MCP ("Application") handles information accessed through its integration with QuickBooks Online.

1. Information We Access

This Application accesses QuickBooks Online accounting data including invoices, customers, vendors, bills, and related financial records solely to fulfill the authorized user's requests.

2. How We Use Information

Data accessed through this Application is used exclusively for the personal and internal business purposes of the authorized user. No data is sold, shared, or disclosed to third parties.

3. Data Storage

This Application does not permanently store QuickBooks Online data. Data is accessed in real time and is not retained beyond the active session.

4. Data Security

Access to QuickBooks Online data is protected via OAuth 2.0 authorization. Only the authorized account holder may grant or revoke access.

5. Third Parties

This Application does not share user data with any third-party services.

6. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Continued use of the Application constitutes acceptance of any changes.

7. Contact

For questions regarding this Privacy Policy, contact the application operator directly.
